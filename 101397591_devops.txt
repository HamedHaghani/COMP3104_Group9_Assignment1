The COMP 3104 DevOps course at George Brown College is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of DevOps principles, tools, and practices essential for modern software development and deployment. The course covers topics such as continuous integration and continuous delivery (CI/CD), version control, automation, and containerization. Students gain hands-on experience with popular tools like Git, GitHub Actions, Docker, and Jenkins, preparing them to streamline workflows and improve collaboration within development teams. By emphasizing real-world applications and industry best practices, the course equips students with the skills to enhance software quality, reliability, and delivery speed, making them valuable assets in the tech industry.
